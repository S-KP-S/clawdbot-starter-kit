### 2. Event Venue
**Revenue potential:** $2,000-15,000+ per event

**Event types:**
- Weddings (highest margin)
- Corporate retreats
- Workshops/classes
- Private dinners
- Photography sessions

**Infrastructure needed:**
- Scenic outdoor area
- Indoor backup space
- Parking (or shuttle)
- Restrooms
- Power/lighting

**Key metrics:**
- 30-50 events/year is realistic
- Wedding venues can gross $100K-500K/year
- Lower maintenance than STR (fewer turnovers)

**Next steps:**

**Revenue potential:** $200-800/member/season

**Models:**
- **CSA (Community Supported Agriculture)** â€” Upfront payment, weekly boxes

**Crops to consider:**

**Key metrics:**

- CSA provides predictable cash flow
- Combine with STR for "farm stay" premium

**Next steps:**

## Land Hacking Strategies

### 1. House Hack + Land
Buy property with livable structure, rent out parts while building.

### 2. Raw Land + Mobile Structure
- Buy cheap raw land
- Place yurt/RV/tiny home
- Build STR income while developing

### 3. Conservation Easements
- Reduce property taxes
- Potential tax deduction
- Protect land long-term

### 4. USDA Programs
- Beginning Farmer Loans (low interest)
- Conservation programs (cost-share)
- Rural development grants

### 5. Seller Financing
- Lower barrier to entry
- Negotiate terms directly
- Good for rural properties
